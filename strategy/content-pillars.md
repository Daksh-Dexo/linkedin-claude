# Content Pillars — Dexo Media LinkedIn

## The Philosophy
Not the 100% everyone already knows.
The 1–2% that's actually useful and hidden.

Content acts as a **filter** — attract founders who think long-term, repel everyone else.
Call out BOTH sides. Don't just criticize agencies. Don't just criticize clients.
Be the person in the room willing to say what no one else will.

---

## Pillar 1: Brand Breakdowns
**What it is:** Real analysis of what brands are doing right or wrong. Not a praise post. Not a takedown. An honest read.

**Who it's for:** D2C founders, marketing managers — people making decisions on brand and spend.

**Angles to explore:**
- A brand spending on awareness when they should fix retention
- Premium creative, weak product — why that doesn't work
- Brands copying creative formats that died 2 years ago
- What a brand's ad account tells you about how they think
- When a brand's positioning and product are completely misaligned

**Format:** Long-form text. Story structure. Name the brand or keep it anonymous depending on sensitivity.

**Frequency:** 1–2x per week

---

## Pillar 2: Agency Reality
**What it is:** Internal truths about how agencies actually work. The stuff nobody posts because it makes them look bad or makes clients uncomfortable.

**Who it's for:** Founders evaluating agencies, young agency folks, other agency owners.

**Angles to explore:**
- What agencies promise in the pitch vs. what the first 90 days look like
- Why I fired a client (and how I knew it was time)
- The onboarding call where I lost a client by being too honest
- How we filter clients before they even get on a call
- The agency killer nobody talks about: great work, terrible communication
- What "we'll scale your brand" actually means operationally
- Why agencies take on too many clients and what it does to quality

**Format:** Long-form text. Failure-first structure. Specific examples. Quiet landing.

**Key rule:** Call out BOTH sides. Agencies overcommit. Clients expect magic. Say both.

**Frequency:** 1–2x per week

---

## Pillar 3: Marketing Truths
**What it is:** What actually works in performance marketing vs. what LinkedIn says. Practical, data-informed, no hype.

**Who it's for:** Founders running ad budgets, marketing managers, anyone tired of recycled frameworks.

**Angles to explore:**
- The metric clients obsess over that I've stopped caring about
- What ₹50K/month in ad spend actually buys you
- AI in marketing: what's useful vs. what's workflow theater
- Why the creative that ran for 11 months worked (and others didn't)
- The attribution problem nobody wants to admit
- What "testing" actually looks like when you're doing it right
- Why most brands don't have a traffic problem — they have a conversion problem

**Format:** Mix of long-form and short punchy posts. Can use data and specific numbers.

**Frequency:** 1–2x per week

---

## Pillar 4: Operator Insights
**What it is:** How Dexo actually operates. SOPs, communication, team structure, scaling problems. The unglamorous stuff that determines whether an agency survives.

**Who it's for:** Other agency owners, early-stage founders building teams, marketing managers managing chaos.

**Angles to explore:**
- The SOP that saved us when a senior person quit mid-campaign
- How we structure client communication to avoid 90% of escalations
- What scaling from 5 to 11 people broke — and what we had to rebuild
- Why communication efficiency matters more than performance efficiency
- How we run internal reviews without them becoming blame sessions
- What a client update should actually contain (and what kills trust)

**Format:** More structured than other pillars. Can use brief bullet points where they serve clarity. Specific and operational.

**Frequency:** 1x per week

---

## Bonus: Personal Story / Win
Not a separate pillar — more of a thread that runs through everything.
Use personal story when there's a real moment worth sharing: a client win, a rejection, a lesson that hit differently.
Keep it grounded. No performance. No "I'm grateful for the journey."
