# Draft: ROAS is not the starting point

Bucket: Marketing Truth
Format: Long-form
Status: Draft — April 8, 2026

---

A client came to me obsessed with one number.

ROAS.

I tried to explain something.

ROAS is not the starting point.
It's the outcome of everything that happens before it.

• Strong CTR → better CPC
• Healthy CPC → more people actually reach your site
• Good landing page views → your traffic quality and website are both working
• Strong Add to Cart → people trust the product
• Consistent Checkout Initiation → your checkout isn't leaking

All of that, together, produces ROAS.

It's not a standalone metric.
It's a result.

---

And here's the part most founders miss.

5x ROAS on ₹20,000 ad spend looks impressive.
It doesn't build a business.

2.2x ROAS on ₹5 lakh spend gives you maybe 5% profit.
But that volume? That sustains. That scales.

Volume and profitability are two different games.
Most people are playing one while thinking they're playing the other.

---

Same client also asked me:
"When will I become profitable?"

Wrong question.

The right question is:
"When will I reach consistency in orders?"

Because in the first 1–2 months, every brand goes through instability.
Some days you get orders.
Some days you get zero.

The goal isn't profitability yet.
The goal is to exit that phase and find a median baseline — where orders become predictable.

You optimize for profitability after that.
Not before.

---

I explained all of this.

They went with another agency.
One that promised better numbers.

That's fine.

I know how this usually plays out.

They'll be back in 2–3 months.
